Saas based Insta Bill

The retail environment is changing rapidly, and that is reflective of more aware customers who are looking for higher convenience, specific products, faster access, and in the current context, a safer environment as well. Thus retailers need to continue to evolve, keeping ‘Customer Centricity’ as the core of the transformation to develop unique and innovative solutions, creating seamless and frictionless experiences for the customers across varied channels. AI technologies like computer vision bring near-real-time intelligence to the stores. The new generation of shoppers, the millennials, expect bespoke shopping experiences in real-time and at the lowest price. They want shorter wait times, quicker order fulfilment, personalized product recommendations & frictionless checkout experiences. This new generation was brought up in a digital world where they can find multiple options with a click of a button.

Vantaige InstaBill cloud solution provides a unique and seamless checkout experience, no more waiting in the queue. Vantaige InstaBill is a Saas based computer vision AI application, which makes billing of the purchased items with just the click of the button . The customer places all the items on the billing counter, the camera on top of it takes a picture of these items and instantly generates the bill. The bill is automatically delivered to the user’s mobile Insta Bill application, which is integrated with payment systems to even pay easily. Further, the solution provides various statistics to the customers to track and analyse their monthly spending helping households to plan their future spending wisely.
